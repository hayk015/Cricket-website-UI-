Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set a match reminder?
Go to the Fixtures page and click the "🔔 Reminder" button on any match card. The button will turn green when the reminder is active. Reminders are saved in your browser.
How do I view team profiles?
Navigate to the Teams page from the top navigation bar. Each team card has a "TEAM PROFILE" button that opens the official ICC page for that team.
Is this an official ICC website?
No, this is a student mini-project inspired by ICC's design. For official information visit icc-cricket.com.
How do I search for a specific team or player?
Use the Search page (accessible from the top nav). Type any team name, tournament name, or keyword and results will filter instantly.
